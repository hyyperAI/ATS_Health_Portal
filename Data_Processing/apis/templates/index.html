MIMIC-IV is a publicly available, cloud hosted, dataset containing the de-identified intensive care information of over 40,000 patients from 2008 to present. The dataset is HIPPA compliant and is sourced from two large US-based hospital facilities.

The dataset includes data relating to countless aspects of patient care and hospital operations including but not limited to radiology, diagnoses, billing, demographics, ICU usage, surgery notes, etc

ICU

Using historical data to train Deep Learning models to predict future ICU usage trends. Predictions can be used to aid resource related decision making.
